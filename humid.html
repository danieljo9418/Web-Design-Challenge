Humidity

Based on the data retrieved in this project, it appears that there may be a weak correlation between latitude and humidity. In both the northern and southern hemispheres, the humidity increased as the latitude increased (r-squared values of 0.37). While a weak correlation may be present, the large variance within the dataset does not indicate that there would be much predictive power in this relationship.
