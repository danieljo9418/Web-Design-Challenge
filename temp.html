Max Temperature

Based on the data retrieved in this project, there does appear to be a correlation between latitude and max temperature. The observable trend in this data set shows that the max temperature tends to increase as the cities became closer to the equator. In the northern hemisphere, the r-squared value was -0.87, indicating a strong relationship. The southern hemisphere did not have as strong a correlation, but with an r-squared value of 0.44, we can conclude that latitude has some predictive quality when it comes to max temperature here as well.
